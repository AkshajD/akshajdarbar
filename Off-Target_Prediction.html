CRISPR Off-Target Prediction Using Deep Learning

About The Project

This project is based on a study conducted by researchers at the City University of Hong Kong. It attempts to improve off-target effect predictions for CRISPR experiments using a CNN and an FNN. Overall, the study found that the two models outperformed all other competitive machine learning models and designed algorithms, showing that deep learning can have a huge impact on gene editing and genomics, and could be the next step in making gene editing accurate and effective enough to be used in healthcare.
