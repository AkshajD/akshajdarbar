About Me

I'm Akshaj. I'm a 16-year old from Kingston, Ontario, obsessed with solving the world's biggest problems, and impacting billions of people.

Currently, the world suffers from many problems, especially in the fields of medicine and environmental care. I want to apply my ever-growing toolset, currently consisting of artificial intelligence and programming development experience, to solve these problems and help billions of people worldwide.

At the same time, I want to continue developing my toolset, in fields such as genetic editing, nanotechnology, and quantum computing to better equip myself to solve problems. These include finding new treatments and cures to cancer, genetic disorders, and the issue of slow machine processing.
